Welcome to Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

At Rexburg Chamber of Commerce, our mission is to foster a thriving business community by connecting, supporting, and empowering local enterprises. This page serves as a hub for our members and the broader community, offering valuable resources, networking opportunities, and insights to promote economic growth.

Chamber Challenge Marathon Extravaganza

The Chamber Challenge Marathon Extravaganza, orchestrated by the Chamber of Commerce, is a dynamic and entertaining event designed to blend physical activity with business-themed challenges. As participants traverse the marathon route, they encounter interactive Business Puzzle Pitstops, engage in an Entrepreneurial Relay Race, and visit Commerce Carnival Stations for festive challenges inspired by local businesses. The event also features a Networking Nook for participants to connect with community leaders and entrepreneurs, an Economic Photo Scavenger Hunt to showcase local landmarks, and an Innovation Sprint where participants pitch creative business ideas. The marathon concludes with a lively Finish Line Celebration, fostering a sense of community pride and highlighting the diverse economic landscape of the region. This unique and fun-filled experience not only promotes health and camaraderie but also strengthens the bonds between the community and its local businesses.

Business Brews & Banter Social Mixer

Welcome to the Business Brews & Banter Social Mixer, a laid-back and enjoyable gathering hosted by the Chamber of Commerce, where local entrepreneurs, business professionals, and community members converge for an evening of lively conversations and connections. The event unfolds in a relaxed venue adorned with tasting stations from local breweries, offering an array of craft brews to set the perfect social backdrop. Attendees engage in Business Bingo, encouraging meaningful discussions, while Entrepreneurial Spotlights and brief interviews provide insights into local success stories. A Community Collaboration Wall facilitates idea-sharing, and structured speed networking rounds ensure diverse interactions. With business trivia, a local artisan showcase, and the overall convivial atmosphere, the Business Brews & Banter Social Mixer offers a refreshing approach to networking, celebrating the collaborative spirit and diverse talents within the local business community.

Legend has it that back in the whimsical year of 1888, a town meeting was called to address the pressing issue of organizing the chaotic marketplace where vendors cheerfully hawked their wares in a lively and colorful array. Enterprising locals, known for their love of commerce and camaraderie, decided that the time had come for a more structured approach. A community potluck was organized at the town square, where townsfolk gathered to discuss the future of Progressville's bustling trade scene. It was during this feast of ideas that Mr. Bartholomew Pennyworth, an eccentric inventor, suggested the formation of a society dedicated to fostering business harmony. The proposal was met with resounding approval, and so, the Great Guild of Progressville Traders was born. With Mr. Pennyworth appointed as the first "Grand Maestro of Commerce," the guild soon evolved into the modern-day Chamber of Commerce. Its inaugural meeting took place in the town's biggest treehouse, where members exchanged not only business insights but also their favorite recipes for peach cobbler. Throughout the years, the Chamber of Commerce in Progressville became a symbol of unity and entrepreneurial spirit, hosting quirky events like the Annual Lively Lumberjack Jamboree and the Great Gopher Golf Gala. The tradition lives on, and today, the Chamber stands as a testament to the lively history and charming eccentricities of Progressville—an emblem of how a community can flourish when guided by a shared love for commerce and a dash of whimsy.
